His Story

A Little Boy With Big Dreams,
And a Family Struggling to Help Him

👁️

Born Into Darkness

Aarav was diagnosed with severe visual impairment at just 3 months of age. His parents, daily wage workers, were devastated — but they never gave up hope. They want him to study, to laugh, to thrive — just like any other child.

🏫

Denied Basic Education

Without Braille textbooks, a trained special educator, or assistive technology, Aarav cannot attend a mainstream school. Specialized schools for visually impaired children exist — but the fees and transport are beyond what his family can manage.

🤝

What Your Help Means

Your donation will fund his enrollment in a special school, Braille learning kits, a talking tablet, and medical assessments. ₹500 buys him a month of learning materials. ₹2,000 covers school fees. Every rupee counts for Aarav.

"When I asked Aarav what he wants to be when he grows up, he said — 'I want to be a teacher, so I can teach other blind kids.' That moment broke and mended my heart at the same time."

— Aarav's mother, Kavitha
